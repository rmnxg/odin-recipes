Lasagna

Description

Today I'm going to teach you how to make an authentic lasagna as if I were an Italian nonna from the 20th century. That is to say, without skimping on time, lover or the quality of the ingredients.

I invite you to see how it's done and prepare it yourselves. Give it a shot!

Ingredients

For the filling

  • 600 g beef
  • 300 g pork belly
  • 1 onion
  • 2 carrots
  • 1 celery branch
  • 200 ml red wine
  • 800 g peeled tomatoes or tomato sauce
  • 3 tbs olive oil
  • salt to taste
  • ground black pepper to taste

For the pasta

  • 200 g common flour
  • 2 big eggs
  • 1-2 tbs olive oil
  • 1 pinch of salt

For the bechamel

  • 80 g butter
  • 80 g common flour
  • 750 ml milk
  • nutmeg to taste
  • salt to taste
  • black pepper to taste
  • 200 g parmesan
